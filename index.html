A TERRIBLE PLAGUE OF TOAD MONKEYS HAS CONSUMED THE STONE FOREST!

The Stone Forest, not far from the Reverse Portal, has become the epicenter of an unheard of disaster. The locals are panicking, trying to find shelter from the sudden a plague of toad monkeys, and local authorities are sounding the alarm.

According to information from the village elders, magic toad monkeys usually live near the tropical streams and rarely venture outside their habitat. However, a few days ago, the village elders started receiving visions of the future about a terrible element. Some kind of unprecedented curse has turned the harmless magical creatures into a veritable plague.

Toad monkeys, which usually feed only on small insects, are now devouring all the stone trees in their path, causing unprecedented terror among the locals. The people who take refuge in caves, watch helplessly as their longtime proud home, the Stone Forest, disappears off the from the face of the Earth.

The help of the strongest wizards capable of dealing with this threat is needed. The vizier of the village declared, “We need wizards who can not only curb these beasts, but also “to rebuild our homeland. We call on allies from all over the world to stand in defense of the Stone Forest! “in defense of the Stone Forest!”
